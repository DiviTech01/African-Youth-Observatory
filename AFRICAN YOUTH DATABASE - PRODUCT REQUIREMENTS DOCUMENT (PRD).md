**1.** **Product** **Overview**

**1.1** **Product** **Name**

African Youth Database (AYD)

**1.2** **Product** **Vision**

To become **Africa’s** **most** **trusted,** **accessible,** **and**
**intelligent** **youth** **data** **infrastructure**, powering policy,
research, innovation, and investment decisions across the continent.

AYD will move African youth data from **static** **reports** **and**
**scattered** **portals** into a **living,** **interactive,** **and**
**decision-ready** **system**.

**1.3** **Problem** **Statement**

Youth data in Africa is currently:

> ● Fragmented across multiple institutions and countries
>
> ● Difficult to access, compare, and visualize
>
> ● Often outdated, static, or poorly contextualized
>
> ● Not easily usable by policymakers, startups, NGOs, or youth
> themselves

There is **no** **single,** **youth-focused,** **continent-wide**
**platform** that combines:

> ● Verified datasets
>
> ● Interactive visualization
>
> ● Comparative insights
>
> ● Policy relevance
>
> ● Trust and provenance

**1.4** **Solution** **Summary**

AYD provides:

> ● Centralized youth datasets for all African countries
>
> ● Interactive charts, graphs, and maps
>
> ● A composite African Youth Index
>
> ● AI-powered insights and narratives
>
> ● Custom dashboards and comparisons
>
> ● Open and premium APIs
>
> ● Partner contribution and verification systems

**5.** **Core** **Features** **&** **Requirements**

**5.1** **Data** **Aggregation** **&** **Management**

> ● Upload, validate, and store youth datasets
>
> ● Metadata: source, year, methodology, geography
>
> ● Versioning and update history

**5.2** **Interactive** **Visualizations**

> ● Animated bar charts, line graphs, and trend charts
>
> ● Hover tooltips showing values and explanations
>
> ● Filters by country, year, theme

**5.3** **African** **Youth** **Index**

> ● Composite index ranking countries annually
>
> ● Indicator-level breakdowns
>
> ● Time-based comparisons

**5.4** **Interactive** **African** **Map**
